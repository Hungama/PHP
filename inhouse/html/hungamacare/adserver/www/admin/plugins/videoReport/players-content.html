Open Ad Streamer for Flowplayer 3.1.3

  • Show video ads served by OpenX in Flowplayer
  • Supports the Video Ad Serving Template (VAST) standard
  • Supports progressive download (http) and streaming (rtmp)
  • Supports pre/mid/post-roll video ads, overlay ads and companion banners
  • Entirely Open Source for both the Ad Server and the Player
